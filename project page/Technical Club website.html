Project Description:

The central hub for all betaLabs related creations, with detailed descriptions about all ongoing and upcoming projects.

Main Features: BlogSpot -
Members can write and read blogs with interactive elements such as likes and comments. Similar concept to Medium. CodeChef and CodeForces API -
Rating based leaderboard system for students of our college. Resources Tab -
Section containing useful articles, youtube videos and sample projects that will shed more light on different domains. Projects Showcase Tab -
This section will provide the overview and links to the detailed view of the projects created by the students of our college. Events Listing -
A timeline of all current and upcoming Technical Club events. Posters Corner -
Showcase of posters pertaining to individual projects, resource for project display on social media. Hall of Fame -
Cherry Picked Exceptional Projects showcasing the best talent that our college has to offer. Time-Keeping Leaderboard -
Leaderboard showing the hours clocked in by volunteers. standalone-leaderboard
