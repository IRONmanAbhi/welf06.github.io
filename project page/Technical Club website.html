Project Description:

The central hub for all betaLabs related creations, with detailed descriptions about all ongoing and upcoming projects.

Main Features:

1. BlogSpot - Members can write and read blogs with interactive elements such as likes and comments. Similar concept to Medium.

2. CodeChef and CodeForces API - Rating based leaderboard system for students of our college.

3. Resources Tab - Section containing useful articles, youtube videos and sample projects that will shed more light on different domains.

4. Projects Showcase Tab - This section will provide the overview and links to the detailed view of the projects created by the students of our college.

5. Events Listing - A timeline of all current and upcoming Technical Club events.

6. Posters Corner - Showcase of posters pertaining to individual projects, resource for project display on social media.

7. Hall of Fame - Cherry Picked Exceptional Projects showcasing the best talent that our college has to offer.

8. Time-Keeping Leaderboard - Leaderboard showing the hours clocked in by volunteers. standalone-leaderboard
